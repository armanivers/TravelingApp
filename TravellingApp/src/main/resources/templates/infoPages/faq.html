FAQ

How can I create a new account?

You can get a new Account, just click here and follow the instructions.

How can I cancel my booking?

You can cancel your booking two weeks before the trip starts.
To do this, you must send a written application to DEAP, and then you get a confirmation from us.

Will I get a confirmation to my book?

Yes, you will receive a confirmation email from us, with all the important information about the booking, invoice and travel dates.

With which payment methods can I pay?

You can pay with:

  1. PayPal
  2. credit card (Mastercard and Visa)
  3. Amazon Payments
  4. payment in installments powered by Amazon

How do I find the ideal hotel for me?

Just use our filter-action, choose your wishes, your asking price and your criteria.

You get vouchers as a gift?

Yes, you can get vouchers. But the voucher must be worth 200 euros.
